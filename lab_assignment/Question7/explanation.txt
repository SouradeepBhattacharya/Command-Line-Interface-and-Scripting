Command: ./patterns.sh input.txt
Explanation: The script converts the input into lowercase words and classifies each word. Words containing only vowels (aeiou) are written to vowels.txt, words containing only consonants to consonants.txt, and words that contain both vowels and consonants but start with a consonant to mixed.txt. It ignores case while checking patterns.
Output:
Words containing only vowels written to vowels.txt
Words containing only consonants written to consonants.txt
Words containing both vowels and consonants (starting with a consonant) written to mixed.txt

Contents of vowels.txt:
a
aeiou

Contents of consonants.txt:
bcdfg
rhythm
crypts

Contents of mixed.txt:
quick
brown
fox
jumps
the
lazy
dog
consonant
vowel
